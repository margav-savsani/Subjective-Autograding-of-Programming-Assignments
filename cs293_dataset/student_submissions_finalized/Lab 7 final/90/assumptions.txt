The trains shall first be arranged based on day, then dep time, then arr time.

The stnInfoList input into Quicksort shall represent a particular train journey, 
that is only one of its bool dayofWeek[7] shall be true

No train shall end at a station where there are further stations ahead, 
that is even if one trains end at a station, then other trains if exist,
shall be ending at this station. Similar is the case with start stations.
