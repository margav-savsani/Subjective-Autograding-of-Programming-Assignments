A train won't stop at a station for more than 24 hrs
A train won't run continuously without stopping for more than 24 hours
TRANSIT TIME is given in number of hours and not in 24hrs format
( eg: 20 and not 2000 )

TRANSIT time is defined as the max time a person has to wait on a station
