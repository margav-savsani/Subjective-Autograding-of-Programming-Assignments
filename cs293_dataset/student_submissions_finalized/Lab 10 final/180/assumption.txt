OUTPUT FORMAT::

* All different possible paths are separated by "==============================".
* In a single path suppose there is journey like A - B - C - D - E - F and there is change in train at C and E then output will be
	 journey info at A (JourneyCode = 100 {assumed})

	journey info at B (JourneyCode 100)

	journey info at C (here there will be changed journeycode as we have changed train)
	
	journey info at D (journeycode same as C)

	journey info at E (some other journeycode again)
