The line printing next to the details of the train are to be done after reaching the next stop
    Eg:

    Day:Sun
    JourneyCode: 331 Arr Time: Starts  Dep: 1900
    Please change to Train with Journey code: 431

    after reaching the next stop we are changing to the train with journey code 431

After reaching the destination we will stop
    Eg:
    
    Day:Sun
    JourneyCode: 331 Arr Time: 830 Dep: 900
    The Next stop With this train is Your Destination

    The next stop of this train is the Destination
    it will stop printing at that point

The inputs maxTransitTime should be given as number of hours
    Eg:5
